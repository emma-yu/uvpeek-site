What we make 能力

01

0 → 1 Products

From research and product definition through to a shipping interface and post-launch iteration. We design every layer of a product — flow, screen, micro-interaction, and the data model behind them.

02

B2B & Console UX

Complex internal systems, dashboards, and SaaS consoles. Onboarding, upgrade paths, empty states, error states, expiry, billing — the parts of a product where most teams give up. We don't.

03

AI Product UX

Designing the trust layer for AI features. How models meet users, what to surface, what to hide, how to set expectation honestly, and how to translate raw capability into something simple, fast, and confident.

04

International Design

Beyond translation. Adapting flow, density, and information architecture to local user habits, cultural expectations, and regional regulation. Multi-region products that feel native, not imported.

05

Research & Strategy

Interviews, surveys, focus groups, heatmap testing, A/B experiments, journey mapping, competitive teardowns. Evidence to back design decisions — not opinion, not vibes, not the loudest stakeholder.

06

Design × Code

Where it makes sense, we ship code too. Native Android (Kotlin / Jetpack Compose), front-end, AI-assisted development. Designs that arrive in production unchanged — because the same person built both.

How we work 方法

01

Listen

Interviews, focus groups, surveys, support tickets. Understand the user before drawing a pixel.

02

Map

Journey maps, information architecture, competitive teardowns. Find the gap, frame the opportunity.

03

Design

Sketches, wireframes, high-fidelity prototypes with motion. Every screen has a reason to exist.

04

Validate

Heatmap testing, A/B experiments, behavioral analytics. Decisions held to the data, not the demo.

05

Iterate

Post-launch retrospectives. The day a product ships is the day the real design starts.
