What we make 能力

01

Build new products

0→1 apps, AI features, products that haven't earned their first user yet. From research and product definition through to a shipping interface and post-launch iteration. We design every layer — flow, screen, micro-interaction, the data model underneath, and the trust layer for AI features (how models meet users, what to surface, what to hide).

02

Fix complex products

B2B consoles, dashboards, SaaS billing, multi-region edge cases — the parts most teams give up on. Onboarding, upgrade paths, empty states, error states, expiry. Backed by research (interviews, heatmaps, A/B, journey mapping), not the loudest stakeholder. International work goes beyond translation: density, hierarchy, and flow adapted to how local users actually work.

03

Ship as one team

Where it makes sense, the same person who designs the interface ships the code. Native Android (Kotlin / Jetpack Compose), front-end, AI-assisted development. Designs arrive in production unchanged because there's no handoff to lose them in.

Why xinxinmingde 差异

Designs that ship — because the designer also writes the code.

Most studios hand off Figma files. We hand off products. The gap between mockup and production is measured in hours, not weeks. No "the engineer didn't quite get the spacing right." If the designer is the engineer, there's no spec to mistranslate.

Decisions held to data, not vibes.

Heatmap tests, A/B experiments, behavioral analytics, post-launch reviews. Every meaningful design choice is testable, and we'd rather be wrong on data than right on opinion. If we can't show you why a screen exists, we shouldn't have drawn it.

We've worked at every scale.

From shipping a personal app on the Play Store to making decisions for platforms used by tens of millions. We bring both ends of that spectrum — the freedom of a startup and the discipline of a system at scale — to your problem.

Senior product designer working alongside your team for a quarter or more. Best for ongoing product evolution, weekly cycles, and teams that need a design partner, not a deliverable.

How we work 方法

01

Listen

Interviews, surveys, support tickets. We mean it — half the brief usually changes once we actually listen.

02

Map

Journey maps, IA, competitive teardowns. Find the gap, frame the opportunity. This is where bad ideas die cheaply.

03

Design

Sketches → wireframes → high-fidelity prototypes with motion. Every screen earns its place.

04

Validate

Heatmap testing, A/B experiments, behavioral analytics. Decisions held to the data, not the demo.

05

Iterate

Post-launch retros, follow-up tweaks, weekly metrics review. The day a product ships is the day the real design starts.
